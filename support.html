Ify Tasks

How to create a new task?

First, click on the "Tasks" page on the nav panel on the left, then go to the bottom of the page, where the task input field is located. Type your task down (it can be text, emoji, numbers, and even other languages supported in UTF-8!), then click on the blue button or the Enter key to create a new task!

How to complete a task?

First, click on the "Tasks" page on the nav panel on the left, then locate the task you wish to complete. Click on the empty circle on the left of the task item and you're done with a ding sound!

How to edit a task?

First, click on the "Tasks" page on the nav panel on the left, then locate the task you wish to edit. Click on the white pen icon on the editing bar on the right of the task item and you can edit the task however you want! Just click on any other place on the screen to save the changes!

How to remove a task?

First, click on the "Tasks" page on the nav panel on the left, then locate the task you wish to delete. Click on the white trash can icon on the editing bar on the right of the task item and you can delete the task from the screen.

How to reorder tasks?

First, click on the "Tasks" page on the nav panel on the left, then click the task you wish to reorder in the task list. Drag the task item with your mouse and there will be a light blue line indicating where the task will be once you drop it. Release your mouse to drop it!

How to save tasks?

Ify autosave all the tasks you create, the changes you edit, and removal of tasks. As long as you're using the same browser profile, the tasks will be saved and you can access them anytime, anywhere!

Ify Notes

How to create a note?

Ify currently doesn't support creating a note, you can only access one note file for now. Changes will be rolling out soon!

How to access a note?

First, click on the "Notes" page on the nav panel on the left, then click on either one of the four notes on the page, they all lead to the same note (you will be able to access different notes and create them real soon!), and you can access the note file!

How to edit a note?

First, click on the "Notes" page on the nav panel on the left, then open the note file you wish to edit. Click on the textarea in the middle of the page, and you can add text, emoji, numbers, and even languages supported in UTF-8!

How to save a note?

First, click on the "Notes" page on the nav panel on the left, then access the note file you wish to edit. Click on the textarea to edit and click on the light blue button that says "Save Note" and the changes will be saved as long as you're accessing the file from the same broser, any time, any where!

How to delete a note?

Ify currently doesn't support creating a note, you can only access one note file for now. Changes will be rolling out soon!
